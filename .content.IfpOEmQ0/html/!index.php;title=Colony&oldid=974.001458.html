Colonies are the most important structures in the game, they are your "main base", so to speak. Every player has one (and only one) and almost everything revolves around your colony. It's up to you how well you think you should defend your colony. Your colony can't be taken away from you, but it can be conquered. As the number of your workers grow, the colony's appearance changes.

[edit] Role Of Colonies

The colony's roles range from production to deployment of forces. Most of the features are listed below.

[edit] Population With Production

The more workers a colony has, the more resources a player will receive per tick. See population for more details. No matter what, your actual workers will never exceed 1,000 unless you boost and buy the workers packages, raze outposts, or disban units to go over this limit. All normal 24 tick based worker production stops at 1000.

[edit] Crystal and Relic Storage

A new colony receives a crystal after gaining 20 power score, which is kept in the colony. As owning a crystal raises your metal and oil income by 0.25 units per crystal per tick for the whole alliance and your general score by 20, it is considered an item of value. This means that other players will try to steal it. This can only be prevented by the initial Protection stage and by recruiting military units to defend against attacks (once protection expires). Unlike crystals, relics cannot be gained by building any particular structure, but are found across the world once they have been placed in the game by the Admin. As relics raise the metal and oil production for each member of the owner's alliance, they are viewed as having greater importance than crystals. But even more importantly, it is the ownership of relics that determines who wins the round, a crucial fact that also results in attempts to steal them from their owners. See Relics & Crystals.
