[edit] Unit Recruiting

To start an army, players must first recruit units, which will later be grouped into mobile squads. Recruiting requires these steps assuming you are at the recruitment menu. To get to the menu follow step 0.

Step 0, Open Menu
0. You can get to the recruitment menu by clicking on the recruitment Orb on your colony (after clicking your colony), or by clicking your "Main Base" squad in your orders menu.
Step 1, Recruit
1. Click on the green arrow to enter the recruiting menu.
Step 2, Unit Type
2. Now select the desired unit type (Soldier, Vehicle or Tank).
Step 3, Weapon Type
3. Choose the desired weapon type for the unit. This can be Concussive, Beam or Explosive.
Step 4, Upgrade
4. Select the unit upgrade (Armor, Damage, or Range).
Step 5, Train Unit
5. Review the selection. Then click Train Unit.
Step 6, Ticks
6. After a number of ticks (2 for soldiers, 4 for vehicles, 6 for tanks) the new unit will be ready for action. Multiple units can be trained at once.

More information can be found on the Military Unit Types and the full list of the 27 possible units on the Military List page.
