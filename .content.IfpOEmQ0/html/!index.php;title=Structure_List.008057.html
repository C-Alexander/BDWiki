This is the full list of structures in Battle Dawn.

The time that it takes to build structures on faster (2 and 3 tick) worlds are cut down accordingly, structures 2 tick worlds take 1/2 the time as a 1 tick world to build, and structures on 3 tick worlds take 1/3 the time to build. Although build times are different, the amount of resources needed in order to build the structure is same across all tick speed worlds.
