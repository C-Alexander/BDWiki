Daily Bonus

From BattleDawn

Jump to: navigation, search

The Daily Bonus runs over a 7 day cycle with a new reward being available each day. The Daily Bonus can be found at the top of the tokens tab in the colony screen. The rewards are:

Day 1 : 220 oil/lumber/gas.

Day 2 : 240 oil/lumber/gas and 220 metal/gold/minerals.

Day 3 : 260 oil/lumber/gas and 240 metal/gold/minerals and 12 energy/mana.

Day 4 : 280 oil/lumber/gas and 260 metal/gold/minerals and 14 energy/mana and 12 workers.

Day 5 : 300 oil/lumber/gas and 280 metal/gold/minerals and 16 energy/mana and 14 workers.

Day 6 : 300 oil/lumber/gas and 300 metal/gold/minerals and 20 energy/mana and 20 workers and 25 Blue tokens.

Day 7 : 120 Blue tokens.


Information regarding the Daily Bonus

  • The Daily bonus has a cooldown of 24 hours after that period you can claim the next Daily bonus however if you do not take the Daily Bonus 48 hours after taking the last Daily bonus it resets to Day 1.
  • After Day 7 the Daily Bonus resets back to Day 1.
  • The Daily Bonus can be taken once per player however it can be taken out on different servers for example Day 1 and 2 on Earth then Day 3 and 4 on Mars.
