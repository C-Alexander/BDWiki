The buildings of the game are where it begins, and without them none of the other aspects of the game are available. This guide considers possible approaches to what order to build them, and how one uses the major buildings - Resource Converter: Level 5, Missile Factor, Teleport Gate, Shield Generator and Ion Cannon.

In What Order Should the Buildings be Built?

The ingame tutorial will take new players through what to build, and advises building in this order:

  1. Build the Farm structure
  2. Build the Metal Mine structure
  3. Build the Oil Mine structure
  4. Build the Barracks structure
  5. Build the Farm: Level 2 structure

This is a good start, however where one goes from it is very debateable. There is no single good way to build your buildings, however there are some reasonable ways to progress. The resource structures will be vital, especially the farm, and so it is wise to get these to level 3 if at all possible. An agressive style will need more than just armour infantry, and so the Military and Workshop classes are vital to this style of player. Getting the level 5 structures each have special effects, which will be dealt with next, however the order with which one builds them is completely circumstantial.

What do each of the Level 5 Buildings do?

Resource Converter: Level 5

The Resource Converter: Level 5 grants the user the ability to transfer resources between players within the same alliance, at a cost of 10 Energy. This has numerous minor uses, but few major uses. Nevertheless its numerous small uses make it a powerful, and often overlooked, building to gain.

During wars large numbers of squads need to be moved on a regular basis, both via gates (see later) and via standard oil-based travel, and this comes at great cost - the ability to send emergency oil or metal, which can if necessary be converted to energy, is vital in keeping the war machine rolling.


Finally after a heavy battle some people will lose greater than others, and this can be a means of evening out the losses that result, allowing the players to balance out a rebuild to recover from the battle as fast as is possible.
