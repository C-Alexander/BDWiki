Chat Rules

1. Keep your messages clean.
2. Do not claim that you are a Tacticsoft staff member if you are not!
3. You may not transmit messages that threatens or harass other players.
4. You may not request another user's account information.
5. Do not post repetitive messages.

And as always, you abide by the Terms of Use Agreement.

How to Start Chat

To start chatting, click on the Start Chat tab, located in the bottom left hand corner of the BattleDawn user interface.

Alliance Chat

To access the Alliance Chat, you must belong to an Alliance. See Alliances.

Each alliance has its own private channel to discuss their own matters.

World Chat

To access the World Chat channel from any other channel, use the command /world

Usage of the World Chat channel allows you to converse with players on your world.
