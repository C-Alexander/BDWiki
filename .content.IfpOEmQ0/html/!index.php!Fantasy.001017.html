Map

Earth is 21,600km tall by 43,200km wide, while Fantasy is only 10,000km x 20,000km. However, Fantasy has very little water space (where colonies and outposts cannot be built), while Earth has a lot (you know, 70% of the surface area).

This does have some impact on the play, even though the rules are the same. With the limited water area, it's not as easy to set up a secure area with nice buffer zones between your alliance and others. At the same time, with the smaller map, it's easier to have influence over more (or even all) of the world.

Troop Types

The chassis types have new names: Infantry is the same in Earth and Fantasy, but instead of Vehicles there are Cavalry and instead of Tanks there are Heavies. Weapon types are also renamed: Slashing instead of Concussive; Piercing instead of Beam; and Crushing instead of Explosive. Otherwise, though, they fight exactly the same as on Earth.

The names and images of the various unit types are all different as well. Below is a table showing the Fantasy names for the different Infantry types.

Resources

On Fantasy worlds, instead of Metal, Oil, and Energy, the resources are Gold, Wood, and Mana respectively.

Notes

Squads->Ships Resources (metal, oil, energy)->(gold, wood, mana) Buildings ->(gold mine, lumber mill, mana stone, Alchemist workshop) Colony->Castle Outposts (Gate, Radar, Silo, TB)->(Portal, Watchtower, Lair, TB) Nukes/Missiles->Dragons Ion Cannon->Firestorm Metal mine->Gold mine; Oil rig->Lumber mill Satellite Scan->Farsight Spell (Infantry, Vehicle, Tank)->(Infantry, Cavalry, Heavy)

Spread Virus

Cost: 20 & 8
Reduces all Infantry in the target to 1 hit point.


Binding Thorns

Cost: 30 & 10
Reduces all Vehicle in the target to 1 hit point.


Earthquake

Cost: 40 & 12
Reduces all Tanks in the target to 1 hit point.


Boo!

Cost: 10 & 4
Sends an event to the owner of the target, revealing that he has a spy.


Make Haste

Cost: 25 & 0
Returns your spy to your colony in 6 ticks, regardless of where the spy is in the world.


Advance Covert Ops

To perform advance covert ops, you need to have a Spy Academy built.


Drain Energy

Cost: 120 & 48
Removes all Energy from the target Colony (Can only be used on colonies).


Burn Oil

Cost: 75 & 48
Destroys 50% of the Oil reserves of the Colony (Can only be used on colonies).


Poison Water Supply

Cost: 100 & 48
Kills 10% of the workers in the Colony (Can only be used on colonies).


Lockdown

Cost: 40 & 8
Prevents squads or nukes from leaving the target for 3 to 6 ticks.
