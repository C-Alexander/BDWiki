[edit] Tokens

Tokens, Red (Supporter Tokens) or Blue (Bonus Tokens), are used to purchase Battle Dawn boosts. Boosts are advantages you can redeem in-game via the "boost" menu in the top left hand corner.

Red/Supporter Tokens: Supporter tokens are tokens that are purchasable via the "Buy Tokens" menu (See the section below for a table of packages). Some boosts can only be bought with these red tokens.

Blue/Bonus Tokens: These tokens are given out to players for certain things. A player receives 100 bonus tokens on completing the tutorial (for the first time only) and gaining 20 power (for the first time only). You can also gain bonus tokens for competing in events, helping Battle Dawn in some way or anything else the administrators deems worthy. You can also earn bonus tokens by referring players to the game, you get 20% of the first token package they buy in bonus tokens and once they reach 20 power in any world you get 50 bonus tokens. Bonus tokens can only be used to buy certain boosts, but can definitely be very useful!

[edit] Red Tokens: Packages

Battle Dawn is a free-to-play game, and relies on supporters to purchase tokens and advertisement for funding. We offer the ability for a player to purchase Red/Supporter tokens if they wish in order to gain the ability to use boosts.

Prices are by the PayPal price, other prices may vary. The prices are correct as of 8th January 2010. All prices in euros.
