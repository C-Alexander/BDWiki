The Resource Converter has the ability to convert one resource into another. There are 4 different ways to transform a particular resource into a different type. In some situations, this can be very useful. The conversion rate seems very low to many and is meant for emergencies. The fifth level of resource converter increases efficiency by 20%


The higher level of Resource Converter structure you build, the more options you have for conversion

Resource Converter Level 1: Allows you to convert at a ratio of 50 : 1
Resource Converter Level 2: Allows you to convert at a ratio of 5 : 1
Resource Converter Level 3: Allows you to convert at a ratio of 100 : 1
Resource Converter Level 4: Allows you to convert at a ratio of 10 : 1
Resource Converter Level 5: Lowers conversion costs by 20%

How to build

Each level of Resource Conversion Structures can be constructed under "Constructions". The cost of each level can be found in the Structure List
