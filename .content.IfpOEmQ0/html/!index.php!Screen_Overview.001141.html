Task Panel

There are 6 parts to the task panel:

  • Messages (Lights up green if there is a new message)
Send messages to, and receive messages from other players here.
  • Events (Lights up green if a new event has happened)
Logs all major activity taken by and against your colony, including scans, Battle Reports and alliance invitations. See Events.
  • Radar (Lights up red if there is a hostile squad incoming)
Displays all visible attacks on you and your alliance's colonies and outposts, as well as the movements of all squads in your alliance. See Radar.
  • Colony
Displays information on your colony, such as structures and resources. See Colony.
  • Community
Displays the current rankings of all colonies and alliances, as well as current bounties, your alliance's page and the locations of all relics. See Community
  • Orders
Allows you to issue movement orders to your squads, as well as view your conquers and outposts. See Orders

Action Buttons

There are 4 action buttons:

  • Find Location
Can be used to find specific players or alliances, or to find coordinates.
  • Satellite Scan
Gives you and your alliance temporary radar coverage in an area at the cost of some energy. See Scans.
  • Build Outpost
Build an outpost at the cost of 500 metal, 300 oil and 15 workers. See Outposts
  • Help
Opens the ingame help menu.

Ruler Information

Displays your avatar and your current military rank, and shows your score and overall rank when rolled over with your cursor.

Token Display

Shows how many tokens you currently have, red for purchased tokens, blue for free tokens.

Resource Bar

Shows your resources; metal, oil, energy and population.

Boost Button

Takes you to the tokens screen, where you can purchase and spend tokens.

Boost Bar

Every time somebody buys a bonus pack with tokens on a server, that server's boost bar increases slightly. When the boost bar reaches 100%, every colony on the server receives a free resource bonus.

Tick Information Bar

Shows the amount of time next until the end of the current tick, the tuck number the server has currently reached, and 4 buttons;

  • A - Takes you to whichever part of the map that the Admin is currently on
  • F - Takes you to the game forums
  • Refresh - Refreshes the client
  • X - Logs you out

Display Buttons

  • Toggle Visibility - opens to Toggle visibility menu, which can be used to remove colonies, outposts, squads, spies, missiles, radar range and relics from your ingame viewer, usually used when an ingame object, like a relic, is obstructing your ability to click something underneath.
  • Find my Spy - Takes you to the current location of your spy.
  • Find my Colony- Takes you to the current location of your colony.
  • Zoom out - Zooms out your map view
  • Zoom in - Zooms in your map view

Map

Shows a map of the ingame world, and which part of it you are viewing in white crosshairs.

Broadcast Bar

Displays all current broadcasts, one at a time. Includes buttons to skip through broadcasts, to write a new broadcast and to delete a broadcast.

Infobar

Gives you a visual confirmation of an action being taken.

Chat button

Starts in-game chat.
